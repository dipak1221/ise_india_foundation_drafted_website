ABOUT RISE INDIA FOUNDATION

We are team of enthusiast from diverse background and registered as non- profit organisation working towards conscientizing the underprivilege citizen.

Vision :- We envision a world of global educated citizens empowered to bring positive change and rid society of it’s evil.

Mission :- To shape a better future through quality education and knowledge of essential life-skills to become self-sustain.

Values :- Rise India Foundation works with defined values of the organisation.

Respect :- We treat all individual with dignity and respect and believe in their potential.

Integrity :- We will maintain moral and ethical principle and adhere code of conduct.

Commitment :- Achieve organization goals with complete and sincere commitment

Creativity :- We support programs that represents creative solutions to community problems.

Transparency :- We will operate openly, candidly and provide full disclosure on issues, projects and practice.
